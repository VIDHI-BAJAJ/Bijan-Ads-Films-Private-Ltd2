AboutUs

Bijan ADS & Films PVT Limited is a company that specializes in advertising and film production. The company provides a range of services related to creating and promoting visual content for various media platforms, helping brands and businesses effectively reach their target audience through compelling storytelling and high-quality production.

Piyush Jaiswal

Director

Piyush Jaiswal is also a director at Bijan Ads and Films Private Limited, working alongside Sanjum Jaiswal to lead the company since its founding. Piyush’s role has been instrumental in shaping the company’s success, particularly in areas such as client relations and project management. His efforts have helped establish the company as a well-respected name in the industry, known for its high-quality production and innovative marketing solutions. Piyush is engaged in additional business activities, contributing to ventures like Benfenli Foods Private Limited and Da Kuund Nature & Resorts LLP, which reflect his wide-ranging business expertise.

Sanjum Jaiswal

Our Director (India)

Sanjum Jaiswal is a director at Bijan Ads and Films Private Limited, where he has played a crucial role since the company's inception on September 2, 1998. With a focus on creative strategy and business development, Sanjum has significantly contributed to the company’s growth in the advertising and film production industry. His leadership has guided Bijan Ads and Films to offer a broad spectrum of services, including branding, direct marketing, and graphic design. Beyond his work at Bijan Ads and Films, Sanjum is also involved in other ventures such as Benfenli Foods Private Limited and Da Kuund Nature & Resorts LLP, showcasing his diverse entrepreneurial interests.
